Voxel Dungeons

What is Voxel Dungeons?

Voxel Dungeons is a game originally conceptualised in the Game Development Society at the Preston Campus of the University of Central Lancashire.

It is a Voxel-based game with RPG elements with features similar to that of Dungeons and Dragons. The intention for this project is to create a game that is similar to a cross between DND and early Maplestory but with a more complex stat system allowing for minimaxing and a strategic playstyle.

We are a team of 7 Computing students at the University of Central Lancashire in Preston, England. We come from many different backgrounds and have an amazing wealth of knowledge between us. The game idea itself is the brainchild of Jack Grover who pitched the idea to our friendship group. Those of us interested joined him and began working on parts of the game in which we had experience or that complemented our chosen course.
